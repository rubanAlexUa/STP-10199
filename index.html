About the Game

Love Matters: Merge Game Story is a touching and engaging merge puzzle game with a romantic narrative twist. Players follow the story of several characters navigating heartbreak, relationships, and self-discovery while solving merging puzzles to unlock memories, rebuild homes, and restore hearts. Through immersive storytelling and elegant visuals, Love Matters combines relaxing gameplay with emotional depth. It’s more than just a match game – it’s a narrative journey where each merge uncovers a fragment of someone’s past or a clue to their future. With a diary-style progression, collectible items, and cinematic storytelling, the game offers players a unique way to experience romantic drama, self-growth, and meaningful choices—all through engaging puzzle mechanics.

Features

  • Narrative Merge Gameplay: Solve merge puzzles to unlock story content and choices

  • Emotional Storylines: Dive into dramatic love stories filled with twists, secrets, and resolutions.

  • Rebuild and Restore: Help characters renovate homes, cafes, gardens, and more by completing merge tasks.

  • Hand-Painted Visuals: Stunning art and soft pastel aesthetics that evoke warmth and nostalgia.

  • Original Soundtrack: Gentle piano melodies and emotional cues bring the story to life.

  • Progressive Chapters: Unlock new story arcs as you merge items and advance.

  • Frequent Updates: New love stories and merge maps are added regularly.

How to Play

Step-by-Step:

  • 1. Merge three identical items to create the next level.

  • 2. Complete merge requests tied to story goals.

  • 3. Collect keys and special items to unlock the next chapter.

  • 4. Decorate story environments and help characters progress emotionally.

  • 5. Watch cutscenes and make occasional choices that affect how the story unfolds.

Chapters

Love Matters is structured into emotional “chapters” instead of traditional levels. Each chapter is a stage in a character’s journey - be it breakup, healing, rediscovery, or romance.

The merge board introduces new items in each arc:

  • Chapter 1:

    The Breakup (merge items like tissues, photo frames, locked phones)

  • Chapter 2:

    The Comeback (journals, yoga mats, motivational books)

  • Chapter 3:

    The New Spark (candles, date clothes, handwritten letters)

The puzzles gradually increase in complexity. You’ll manage board space, merge chains, and timed boosters - while unlocking beautiful environments and emotional content.
