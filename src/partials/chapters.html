Chapters

Love Matters is structured into emotional “chapters” instead of traditional levels. Each chapter is a stage in a character’s journey - be it breakup, healing, rediscovery, or romance.

The merge board introduces new items in each arc:

  • Chapter 1:

    The Breakup (merge items like tissues, photo frames, locked phones)

  • Chapter 2:

    The Comeback (journals, yoga mats, motivational books)

  • Chapter 3:

    The New Spark (candles, date clothes, handwritten letters)

The puzzles gradually increase in complexity. You’ll manage board space, merge chains, and timed boosters - while unlocking beautiful environments and emotional content.
