Features

  • Narrative Merge Gameplay: Solve merge puzzles to unlock story content and choices

  • Emotional Storylines: Dive into dramatic love stories filled with twists, secrets, and resolutions.

  • Rebuild and Restore: Help characters renovate homes, cafes, gardens, and more by completing merge tasks.

  • Hand-Painted Visuals: Stunning art and soft pastel aesthetics that evoke warmth and nostalgia.

  • Original Soundtrack: Gentle piano melodies and emotional cues bring the story to life.

  • Progressive Chapters: Unlock new story arcs as you merge items and advance.

  • Frequent Updates: New love stories and merge maps are added regularly.
