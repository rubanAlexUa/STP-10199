About the Game

Love Matters: Merge Game Story is a touching and engaging merge puzzle game with a romantic narrative twist. Players follow the story of several characters navigating heartbreak, relationships, and self-discovery while solving merging puzzles to unlock memories, rebuild homes, and restore hearts. Through immersive storytelling and elegant visuals, Love Matters combines relaxing gameplay with emotional depth. It’s more than just a match game – it’s a narrative journey where each merge uncovers a fragment of someone’s past or a clue to their future. With a diary-style progression, collectible items, and cinematic storytelling, the game offers players a unique way to experience romantic drama, self-growth, and meaningful choices—all through engaging puzzle mechanics.
