How to Play

Step-by-Step:

  • 1. Merge three identical items to create the next level.
  • 2. Complete merge requests tied to story goals.
  • 3. Collect keys and special items to unlock the next chapter.
  • 4. Decorate story environments and help characters progress emotionally.
  • 5. Watch cutscenes and make occasional choices that affect how the story unfolds.
